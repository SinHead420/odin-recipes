Pizza

Description:

A simple homemade pizza recipe with crispy crust, tomato sauce, melted cheese, and your favorite toppings. Perfect for a quick and tasty meal.

Ingridients:

  • 250g flour
  • 150ml warm water
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F)
  2. Roll out the dough on a flat surface
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella on top
  5. Bake for 10–12 minutes until golden brown
