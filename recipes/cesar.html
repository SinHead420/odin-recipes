Caesar Salad

Description:

A classic Caesar salad with crisp lettuce, creamy dressing, croutons, and parmesan cheese. Light and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 200g lettuce
  • 50g croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing
  • Chicken breast (optional)

Steps:

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce
  2. Grill or cook chicken (optional)
  3. Mix lettuce, croutons, and dressing
  4. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese
  5. Serve fresh and enjoy
