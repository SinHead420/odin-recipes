Burger

Description:

A juicy homemade burger with a soft bun, grilled beef patty, cheese, and fresh toppings. Perfect for a quick meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 burger bun
  • 1 beef patty
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce and tomato
  • Ketchup or burger sauce

Steps:

  1. Grill the beef patty until cooked through
  2. Lightly toast the burger bun
  3. Add the patty and top with cheese
  4. Add lettuce, tomato, and sauce
  5. Serve warm and enjoy
